About Me

I received the B.Eng. and M.Eng. degrees in computer science and technology from Xidian University, Xi'an, China, in 1993 and 1998, respectively, and the Ph.D. degree from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, in 2005. From 1993 to 2000, I was a Lecturer and a Researcher with the School of Computer Science and Technology, Xidian University. From 2000 to 2003, I was a Research Student with the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University. Since 2004, I have been with the School of Computer Science, Fudan University, Shanghai, China, where I am currently a Professor. My current research interests include computer vision, machine learning, pattern recognition, and robotic tasks.
