About Me

I received the M.S. degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Guangzhou University, Guangzhou, China, in 2020. I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree at the School of Computer Science, Fudan University, Shanghai, China. My research interests include computer vision, machine learning, and pattern recognition.
