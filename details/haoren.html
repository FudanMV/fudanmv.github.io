About Me

I received B.Eng. degree in software engineering from the Zhejiang University of Technology in 2017. I am currently with the Shanghai Key Lab of Intelligent Information Processing, School of Computer Science, Fudan University, Shanghai, China. My research interests include pattern recognition and computer vision.
